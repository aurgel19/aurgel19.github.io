Aianne Urgel

Software Engineer and Game Developer

I am a self-motivated and driven software engineer and game developer who is passionate about the ability to tell meaningful stories through video games. My interest in tackling the unique challenges inherent to creating impactful games led to me pursuing a career in the game industry. As a software engineer, I've honed the skill of implementing complex features by breaking big problems down into bite-sized pieces and knowing how to ask the right questions. As a game developer, I am skilled at analyzing how various elements of a game can contribute to its overall experience. Video games have incredible potential as a storytelling medium and I strive to create amazing experiences for players that inspire them to create even more games that tell even more amazing stories.

Personal Projects

Scrap Fishing

A game about a lonely boy collecting scrap to build a robot friend. Created as a solo project.

  • Designed different types of heavy-duty magnets for the player to control; each magnet has different depth and weight limits which allow the player to descend deeper into the scrapyard
  • Created a scrap spawning system where different types of scrap can be found and collected based on the player's current depth
  • Developed an upgrade system where the player can use collected scrap to upgrade their magnet or build pieces of their robot friend

Soup for the Spirits

A game where the player is a chef who makes soup for recently deceased spirits. Created as a solo project to practice creating dialogue with Yarn Spinner.

  • Created a dialogue system with Yarn Spinner which displays conversations between the chef and the spirits
  • Implemented a recipe system where players can cook different types of soup by clicking and dragging combinations of ingredients into a pot

Not Enough Space

A game created as a solo project where the player must manuever their rocket through a series of caverns to reach a landing pad.

  • Designed control scheme for rocket where player must fight against gravity by controlling the rocket thrusters while steering
  • Constructed levels with various hazards the player must avoid such as tight cavern walls and meteors

High Tide

A platformer game where the player must escape from a flooding cave. Created as a solo project to practice implementing platformer movement mechanics.

  • Implemented various kinds of movements in the player controller such as running, jumping, and climbing ladders
  • Built levels with hazards such as spikes, slimes, and steadily rising water that can kill the player
